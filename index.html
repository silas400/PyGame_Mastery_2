Step 1 of the gravity problem!

gravity += some_value

player.y += gravity

This applies a constant force pushing the user downward.

Step 2: Stop the playing from falling through the ground

if player.bottom > y_value:

player.bottom = y_value

This pretty much validates a floor

Step 3: Jump!

event.key = pygame.K_SPACE:

player_gravity = -20

When you make the player go up like this while their is a constant force pushing down on them at the same time, it simulates a jump!
